Temperature Map

Here we see a visualization of the max temperature over a number of cities located at different latitudes.
This dataset was collected on January 05, 2017. During this time of year the Northern hemisphere (latitude > 0°)
is in the winter season, so the max temperature falls as we go father north (ie- as latitude increases). As we
approach the equator, which is closest to the sun in the winter, the temperature increases. There don't seem to
be any cities located below -60°, perhaps corresponding to the dearth of people living in the antarctic circle.
