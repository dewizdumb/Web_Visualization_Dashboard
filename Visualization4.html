Wind Map

Here we see a visualization of the windspeed on January 5, 2018 over a number of cities located at different latitudes.
This visualization shows no discernable pattern of wind speeds according to latitude. The day seems largely calm for
most cities, with most gusts ranging from 0 to 10 miles per hour. The highest wind speed caught on this particular
day in these cities was just over 35 MPH.
